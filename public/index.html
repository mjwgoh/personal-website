Michael is currently a Consumer Mentor at Quest Ventures, where he partners with relentless disruptors, exceptional visionaries, and creative change-makers. Prior to this, he was an Engineering Fellow and Investor, also at Quest.

He started his career founding and working at several venture-backed early-stage startups. His projects and perspectives have been cited in publications including The Business Times, Asia Insurance Review, Zaobao, TNW, and Marketing Magazine. He has also been invited to speak at a number of institutions, including at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore Management University, Citigroup, and Horasis.

Outside the startup ecosystem, he has spent time in management consulting and finance. He has backpacked across Southeast Asia and is working towards becoming a certified glider pilot. Passionate about impact, he can also be found supporting entrepreneurship-related, educational access, and mental health causes.

Michael earned his Bachelor of Arts in Economics and Management from the University of Oxford, where he was a college scholar.
